### 1. Sentinel-5P TROPOMI NO2 Industrial Emissions Layer (Major Feature)

**Connector:** `src/ingestion/sentinel_no2.py` (373 lines)
- Queries CDSE Sentinel Hub Processing API (OAuth2 client credentials)
- Polls all 18 cobalt mines + refineries daily for tropospheric NO2 column density

### Key Intelligence Observations

1. **Moa JV (Cuba)** — reported "paused Feb 2026" but shows non-zero NO2 (1.1x) AND 1 thermal day. Two independent satellite signals showing activity at a "paused" facility. Soft red flag.
2. **Voisey's Bay** — only EMITTING facility. Remote location = low background = clear signal. Consistent with reported operations.
3. **Refineries have higher absolute NO2 than mines** — smelting produces more emissions. Confirms NO2 complements thermal (which misses enclosed refineries).
4. **Chinese facilities** — highest absolute NO2 but ratios ~1.0x due to surrounding industrial pollution. Ratio approach can't distinguish individual facilities in dense zones.
5. **Resolution limitation** — S5P at 5.5km is regional, not facility-level. Best for isolated facilities. The 2.0x threshold may need lowering to 1.5x for better sensitivity.
